    About

    DailyDots is a simple yet powerful task management web app designed to help you stay organized, focused, and productive—one dot at a time.

    With DailyDots, you can:
    -Create tasks and subtasks to break down your day into manageable steps.
    -Mark tasks as complete with a custom, intuitive checkbox.
    -Edit or delete tasks easily to keep your to-do list up to date.
    -Track your progress with tasks saved automatically in your browser for quick access anytime.

    Whether you’re managing daily chores, work projects, or personal goals, DailyDots keeps your tasks organized in a clean, simple, and visually neat layout.
    “One dot at a time” — focus on completing small steps to achieve big goals.
